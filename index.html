Why Choose Us

The Apex Flex difference

Deep Singapore market knowledge
Our consultants live and work in Singapore. We understand local hiring norms, MOM EP/S-Pass regulations, and realistic salary benchmarks across all levels.
Specialist, not generalist
We focus on a defined set of industries so our networks run deep. You get consultants who genuinely know your sector and the people in it.
Honest, transparent communication
We tell you what's realistic on timelines, salaries, and talent availability. No over-promising, no wasted time — just straight talk and good guidance.
Post-placement support
Our relationship doesn't end at an offer letter. We check in at 30, 60, and 90 days to ensure both employer and candidate are set up for success.
MOM licensed and compliant
We operate as a fully licensed Employment Agency under Singapore's Ministry of Manpower — giving you peace of mind on every placement.
